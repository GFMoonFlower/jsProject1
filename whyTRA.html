Supporting Spark's brand-led transformation
+
Spark case study

TRA has been Spark’s strategic insights partner since 2015. During that time, Spark has transformed its brand and vision, lifting from a traditional telco to a much wider digital services provider, all while moving to Agile. Core to our relationship is ongoing brand tracking - Market Monitors that feed into brand and communications planning, optimisation and evaluation.

Beyond this, Spark leverages the full spectrum of TRA’s capabilities, including qualitative and quantitative research, data curation, and cultural expertise. We take a collaborative approach to this partnership, working closely with the entire Spark brand portfolio and their supporting agencies.

“Their collaborative, agile approach helps keep insights relevant an accessible and a highly strategic team is valued across our business.” - Hannah Bay, Domain Chapter Lead, Brand, Spark New Zealand

Shaping the future: Trade Me's fashion market for young people
+
Trade Me case study

Trade Me, a pioneer in NZ online trading since 1999, asked TRA to help them understand 16 - 26 year old's relationship with fashion and retail, so they could shape an impactful offering tailored to their needs.

Our team took a cultural trends approach, paired with extensive digital qualitative immersion. Through nearly 70 videos, our participants expressed themselves through thrift haul videos, mood boards, and wishlists.

The research identified  significant opportunities for Trade Me, from product development, internal and external communication strategies to improve relevance with this valuable demographic.

With a newfound understanding, Trade Me was poised to adapt and develop offerings aligned with their discerning tastes and preferences.

Coopers: repackaging an iconic Australian brand
+
Coopers case study

The brand assets for Coopers, an independent, family-owned brewer since 1862, has been relatively untouched for over 25 years.

For established brand drinkers Coopers stands for authenticity and reliability, but the next generation of beer drinkers are different. The challenge was to identify the sweet spot between maintaining connections with existing audiences, whilst building relevance with new audiences. No easy task in a tightening and highly competitive beer landscape..

A collaborative project with TRA, design agency Cowan, and Coopers set out to identify those distinctive brand assets that would retain the core brand value for loyalists while exploring the fresh assets that would appeal to new drinkers. Finally, assessing the in-situ impact enabled optimisations.

Coopers has successfully rolled out the refreshed pack design for the Coopers Ale range maintaining a top-5 beer brand position and sales in a declining category, and with uplifts in awareness and brand perceptions with their target audience.
