TRA is an insights agency in Australia and New Zealand, we practice the art of knowing people.

Our team approaches complex problems through the lenses of human science. At the intersections, overlaps and margins of these lenses, we see things others don’t. The new ideas that take us from the everyday to the exceptional. The uncommon truths.
